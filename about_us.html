About Us

Situated within 4 km away from the athurugiriya interchange which provides easier access to the airport or a journey to the down south of sri lanka. As our hotel is surrounded by a calm environment of paddy fields, this would be the great place to visit and spend your time or for a quick weekend getaway. We are more than happy to host any event that you desire and to help plan and organize the event from start to end.

SEWAGE TREATMENT AND WASTEWATER MANAGEMENT

Hotel Managed with the Modern Sewage Treatment and Waste Management system from JAPAN to recycle and dispose of the waste. Our belief is conservation and protection of the environment.

SOLAR RENEWABLE ENERGY

As resources such as oil and coal are reducing, we use solar panels to generate energy and supply to the main grid to reduce our energy usage and to reduce our carbon footprint for a greener and eco friendly world.
